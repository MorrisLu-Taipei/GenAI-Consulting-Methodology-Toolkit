## 7. Common Client Question: Does This Conflict with Our Methodology?

### 7.1 Client uses TOGAF / Zachman

**No conflict**. Tiger AI 4 layers **align directly with TOGAF BDAT** (Business/Data/Application/Technology). Say: "We build on your existing TOGAF architecture, adding the GenAI adoption dimension (L1-L5) and quarterly radar closed loop."

### 7.2 Client uses ITIL

**No conflict**. Tiger AI Stage 8 Audit Log / Permission Matrix / Risk Register connects directly to ITIL Service Operation. Say: "We supplement GenAI-specific LLM call logs, Reviewer simulation, Skill version governance."

### 7.3 Client uses CMMI

**No conflict**. Tiger AI L1-L5 and CMMI 5 levels **are kin** — both maturity capability school extensions. Say: "CMMI for AI is an emerging direction; Tiger AI L1-L5 is one landing version."

### 7.4 Client uses BCG / McKinsey / Bain internal frameworks

**No conflict, mutual reinforcement**. Tiger AI cites these firms' core tools (Issue Tree, MECE, Pyramid, Bain Way). Say: "We don't replace your strategic consulting methodology; we add the GenAI adoption closed loop and 4-layer Reference Model."

### 7.5 Client uses Gartner / Forrester AI Maturity

**No conflict, more concrete**. Tiger AI L1-L5 has **more concrete landing tools** than Gartner's 5 levels (L1=OpenWebUI, etc.). Say: "Gartner says 'Operational AI'; we say 'n8n Workflow 3 live + Reviewer sign-off rate 95%'."

## 9. Closing: Standing on Giants' Shoulders + Completing the Industry Closed Loop

Tiger AI methodology design philosophy:

1. **No reinventing wheels**: strategy analysis (McKinsey), change mgmt (Kotter), financial tools (NPV/IRR), EA frameworks (TOGAF/Dragon1) — use the best of industry
2. **Integration + closed loop**: tie scattered tools into a complete reasoning chain with data dependencies, client signatures, and falsification mechanisms
3. **Fill GenAI gaps**: industry frameworks haven't caught up with LLM/Agent/Workflow era → Tiger AI L1-L5 + 4-layer + Cases-as-Benchmarks + HITL design fills it
4. **Reproducible by others**: Apache 2.0 + GitHub + clear academic citations → not any firm's private asset

> **This is the meaning of "standing on giants' shoulders + completing the industry closed loop"** — what clients buy is industry-best integration + GenAI-era completion, not any single firm's proprietary method.
